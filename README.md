## 😊 High Density OnePage Math Notes (A4)
For Computational and Discrete Mathematics

These are my OnePage Math Notes that I used for my Computer Science Exams, and they are free to use, print, and share for Computer Science students. I only ask that the metadata and reference markers remain intact to verify the document’s origin and authenticity. 

### 🖨️Printing Notes: 
Be sure to set the Printer setting to Fill the Page to ensure that no further border space is wasted during printing

### ⚙️Features:
- Detailed Handwritten descriptions covering almost every topic tested
- Added Past Exam question examples
- Timetable added for non-calculator Exam (Discrete Math)
